About Me

Hi! I’m Cris, a game designer and programmer currently studying Computer Science at Constructor University. I’ve been passionate about games and creating them since I was young, and over the years I’ve worked on a variety of projects—ranging from solo pixel games to Unreal Engine prototypes.

I love experimenting with new mechanics, AI behavior, and game systems, and I’m always looking for ways to grow both creatively and technically. I also enjoy making tutorials to help others learn and have even built websites like this one to showcase my work.

If you have any questions or just want to talk about game design, feel free to reach out! :)
