In this course, I explored the fundamentals of game development, including game design principles, 2D and 3D graphics, and physics-based mechanics. I worked with tools like Unity (using C#) and the Love2D framework, applying functional programming concepts with Lua. Through hands-on projects, I developed small games that focused on gameplay mechanics, user interaction, and design iteration. The course also provided insights into industry practices, emphasizing the creation of engaging and optimized player experiences.
