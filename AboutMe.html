SKVOT - Introduction to Game Design - Completed

I completed the **SKVOT Introduction to Game Design** course, taught by a **Lead Game Designer at Gameloft Studios**. The course provided a deep dive into **game design principles**, covering topics such as **level design, aesthetics, game mechanics, and player experience**. We explored various methods for designing and prototyping game ideas, particularly using **Unreal Engine**. The course also emphasized the importance of balancing mechanics, creating engaging gameplay loops, and understanding player psychology in game development.
